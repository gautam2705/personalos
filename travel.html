🚦 Universal Rules

My defaults, whatever the cuisine.
  • Order protein first. Build the plate around it.
  • Always ask for sauces on the side. Saves 300+ kcal per meal.
  • One starch, not three. Rice OR bread OR fries — pick one.
  • Water with meals. No sugary drinks, no fruit juices.
  • No dessert by default. If the dessert is exceptional, share one.
  • Walk 10 min after. Post-meal walk = better glucose response.

📋 Pre-Trip Checklist

Logistics to tick off before every trip.
  • Check passport validity (≥ 6 months).
  • Confirm visa & entry requirements.
  • Notify bank of travel dates.
  • Download offline maps + Google Translate language pack.
  • Screenshot hotel address + booking confirmation.
  • Set auto-pay for any bills due during trip.
  • Share itinerary with next-of-kin.
  • Load travel insurance policy on phone.
  • Check weather for entire trip duration.
  • Set out-of-office email and calendar block.
